Technology & Hosting Services 

We were able to use Shopify stores to set up the shop. It was easy to use and quick to implement an 
application. It comes with themes that are responsive and beautiful. It is excellent for selling online and 
on social media, which is what Handmade By Meech is doing currently on Instagram instead, she can have 
a professional store to further her endeavors as a business owner. 

Finally, important items like; marketing, payments, checkout, and shipping are all handled by a trusted 
operator (Shopify). This gives a lot of confidence to independent store owners in that they have an 
established name in the community backing their shop. For hosting services, we had several options but 
finding something affordable required a lot of research. Since the eCommerce website was built on 
Shopify so using their hosting services was easier for us. They offer the domain and the hosting services 
for approximately $9 a month inclusive.  

Payment Methods 

The payment methods implemented for use are AMEX, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Mastercard. Visa, Shop Pay, 
and PayPal. Fees for all applicable payment methods are as follows; credit card rate of 2.9% + CA $0.30 
fee and 0% transaction fee.
