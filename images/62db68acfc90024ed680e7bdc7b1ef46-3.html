Introduction 

For our term project, we decided to help a real business with their eCommerce needs. Handmade By 
Meech is a scrunchie business born on Instagram. Taylor Meechan the founder of the business from 
Wilfrid Laurier is a thriving entrepreneur producing designer scrunchies and currently selling it through 

her Instagram page ‘@handmadebymeech’. Our team is helping her grow her business by providing an 

eCommerce website so she can have better customer reach and professionalism. The website outlines her 
story and the unique product, differentiating what is available in big-box retailers, along with purchasing 
options from her product line. 
 
Her current form of billing is informal because Instagram does not currently support features which she 
needs for her business. The way the business is run at this time is, she accepts direct messages from 
customers on Instagram and payments are through E-transfers. The website, which was developed 
through Shopify will help track sales, future forecasting, create invoices, refunds, merchandising, and 

marketing analytics; all of this will be used to drive her business’s growth.

 

Project Description 

To develop an eCommerce website where products and/or services a small business owner can list their 
offerings to be bought through the Internet, manage invoices/sales, sales projections, merchandising and 
returns. The final product will be developed in these three stages. First, the information stage from both 
the technical standpoint and from the small business owner standpoint. This is where the needs of the 
project and the business owner are coordinated into wireframe planning to organize everything that 
needs to be accomplished. Next, the development phase where the actual e-commerce website will be 
developed using the tools decided in the first stage or with updates accordingly to fit the constraints or 
limitations of the project and its timeline. The last stage is the presentation stage of the final product to 

both the small business owner and for the class. The goal of the website was to highlight Taylor’s unique 

product, it is differentiated by the colors, design and materials which all combine for a stylish accessory 
not currently available in the market.
